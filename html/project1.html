The 2010s!

iPad of a 10 year old with unsupervised internet access

My first generation iPad was the love of my life during childhood.
I couldn't take photos on it but I still had the most fun time exploring the internet with no one to stop me.
I remember filling up my storage with at least 10GB of games, my dad made me delete most of them and I cried.
This website would be a representation of what my iPad looked like back then!
Unfortunately most of these games are gone now so I can only look back at these and be happy I got to experience them.
